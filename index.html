About The MeetUp

Zopiy, the Annual Digital Conference, is your gateway to the latest Digital innovations and strategies. Join us on December 10-12, 2023, at The Bridgewater Hall in the vibrant city of Manchester. This three-day MeetUp is a must-attend for Digital professionals, promising a dynamic blend of insights, innovation, and networking opportunities.

MeetUp Schedule

Over the course of three days, Zopiy attendees will immerse themselves in a world of Digital excellence. Day one kicks off with a focus on branding and consumer engagement, exploring strategies to captivate audiences. Day two delves into data-driven Digital, unveiling the power of analytics and insights. Day three concludes the conference with a comprehensive session on emerging trends in social media Digital, offering a glimpse into the Digital landscape of tomorrow.

MeetUp Venue

MeetUp venue location info and gallery

The Bridgewater Hall, Manchester

The Bridgewater Hall, situated in the heart of Manchester, is a stunning architectural masterpiece and an iconic cultural hub. With its state-of-the-art acoustics and elegant design, it provides the perfect backdrop for Zopiy, offering a world-class setting for our Annual Digital Conference.
